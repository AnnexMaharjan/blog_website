    Place Comments
    Manhattan, New York, USA A bewildering place. There’s such energy here – perhaps a leftover from the oodles of energy that were required to build up such a unique city! I really recommend a helicopter excursion over the borough. You won’t regret it.
    Tokyo, Japan It’s another world – in all dimensions. Especially when the cherry trees are in blossom.
    Kyoto, Japan The former capital of Japan, full of ancient temples and gardens. Don’t miss the Samurai Village where you can get dressed up in full warrior/geisha garb – surprisingly unforgettable fun!
    Machu Picchu, Peru The forgotten city of the Incas. I was there not long ago.
    Sydney, Australia Beautiful bay, the Opera House, the yachts, Bondi Beach, flying foxes… Overall a most charming and pleasant city to visit
